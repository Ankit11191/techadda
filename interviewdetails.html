   Tips for Resume Sending to Recruiters

1. Your email address needs to be professional.
2. Send it in a Morning, ideally well between 9 am-10 am.
3. You should stick to the standard format for subject lines:
      - Subject: ‘Job application’ – Job title, Job ID (if applicable) — Your Name.
4. Email body for sending a resume: Keep it crisp, yet formal.
5. Resume and cover letter need to be sent as attachments.
6. Name your attachments in a way that makes them easy to find.
7. Save documents in PDF format.
8. Files should not be larger than 1MB.
9. Avoid coloring and Emojis.
10.Provide all the professional details in the mail as asked in JD.
