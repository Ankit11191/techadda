Hello, I'm Ankit Mittal, a Technical Manager based in Bangalore, India, with a master's degree from DAVV University, Indore. My professional journey began as a Test Trainee, and through dedication and continuous learning, I have advanced through diverse roles including Manual Tester, Automation Tester, Framework Support, Automation Developer, Project Lead, and Product Lead.

My passion for teaching started early and has grown into a mission to empower others. Over the past 6+ years, I have trained more than 1350 students in various domains of software testing and development. Many of my trainees come from non-technical backgrounds or have significant career gaps, and I take pride in helping them achieve their professional goals.

I have collaborated with reputed training institutes, often volunteering to support students who are financially challenged but driven by ambition. Seeing my students succeed and find job satisfaction is my greatest reward, especially those who have restarted their careers after personal breaks.

Beyond my professional and teaching endeavors, I am passionate about travel, photography, and badminton, which help me stay energized and creative.

Connect with me for mentorship, training, or collaboration opportunities on GitHub, Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram, Gmail, or WhatsApp.
Let's connect, learn, and grow together!
